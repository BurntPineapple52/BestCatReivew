If you have cats then chances are you also have some ugly cat furniture that just doesn’t fit with your home design. Cat furniture like scratching posts, cat trees and litter boxes are essential to keep your cat enriched and happy. These are necessities for cats, so how can we integrate them into our homes and still look stylish?

We’ve scoured the internet looking for the best modern cat furniture that isn’t ugly. Check out our top finds below!

Best Modern Cat Trees

  1. Bogarel CORK CONSTELLATION TREE

The Cork Constellation Tree by French design company Bogarel is exceptional. If you are serious about design, then this is the cat tree for you and your cat. The Constellation design cork cat tree is made up of two half-spheres and two cork trays from the Dehesa forest in Portugal and a chromed steel structure made in Italy. It is made with 100% European and recyclable materials. FSC guaranteed ash and cork: from protected forests.

2. MiaCara Torre Cat Scratching Post Cat Tree

The MiaCara Torre Cat Scratching Post Cat Tree was designed by Dutch designer Regina Mol. Miacara products are manufactured in Germany and Europe with great attention to detail and sustainability. This cat tree will blend in with even the most high end furniture. It’s made with bent plywood and felt; two classic materials heavily featured in Dutch design.

3. Tuft And Paw Sprout Cat Tree

This sculptural cat tree from Tuft and Paw is beautifully designed. It’s made with bent plywood in walnut or ash veneer and soft felt. This cat tree is stunning to look at but also very stable and durable thanks to its construction. It’s slim profile allows you to place it against a wall or window without the bulkiness you get from a standard cat tree.

Best Modern Cat Shelving

Dote was recently founded by London-based designers Nic Wallenberg and Helena Hedenstedt. Dote is on track to be completely sustainable. All of their products are vegan and are either partly or entirely made from recycled materials. The products are not available yet, but you can sign up to be added to their waitlist. In our opinion these are well worth the wait!

2. ModCatShop Umile Modular Cat Set

ModCatShop is a modular cat furniture company that creates all of their designs with sustainable materials. They are handmade in the USA. The Umile Modular Cat Set is a wall mounted cat tree that offers plenty of space for jumping, playing and lounging. It’s a fully equipped cat playground that is both functional and beautiful.

3. Tuft And Paw Plateau Modern Cat Perch

This cat wall shelf is beautifully designed. It’s shaped out of a single piece of wood with a cozy felt interior. It looks stylish and modern when mounted on your wall. Your cat will definitely be lounging in style. The installation is super easy, your feline friend can be enjoying a new view in minutes!

Best Modern Litter Boxes

  1. Cat Person Skylight Litter Box by Jason Wu

This litter box was designed by iconic designer, Jason Wu. This litter box is sleek and minimal, but highly functional. The enclosed litter box ensures your cat’s mess stays inside, but there is plenty of ventilation to keep your cat happy and healthy.

2. Tuft and Paw Nook Litter Box

The Nook Litter Box from Tuft and Paw is the ultimate sleek and simple design. It’s made out of aluminum with a removable top to make for easy cleaning. Many of the covered litter boxes on the market are made out of wood. We don’t recommend this as the wood can absorb waste becoming very worn over time. That’s why aluminum is such a fantastic option!

3. Modkat XL Litter Box

This litter box has to be our top pick for functionality! It’s the largest top entry litter box on the market. Not only can your cat enter from the top but also from the side making it fantastic for cats of all ages. This litter box has a reusable liner inside to keep your litter box clean. It also comes with a scoop that attaches to the side.

Best Modern Cat Beds

  1. E&Y Hammock Table- KOICHI FUTATSUMATA

This table should be on every cat owners list! It’s designed to function as a coffee table and cat hammock all in one. It’s made with a steel base, glass top and molded rattan hammock. This is the perfect solution for stylish integrated cat furniture.

2. Bludot Signal Small Dog Bed

Ok we know this bed is made for dogs but we think your cat will love it just as much! This bed comes in 5 different subtle color/material options, including velvet. Its two toned color adds a wonderful dimension. It is filled with a mix of fiber and down so your cat can get the ultimate in beauty rest.

3. Tuft and Paw Puff Cat Bed

If your cat is the lounging type, then this is the one. The material is super fluffy and cozy but still looks refined. The bed is made out of contoured foam so your cat is effortlessly cradled. We only wish this came in human size!
